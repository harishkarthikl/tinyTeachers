This page is designed to show computers can learn by seeing markings. Are you ready to teach this machine to see? The window below shows the video of your computer's webcam (aka what the machine's eyes). Get a whiteboard and a marker and make your own doodles. You have to show the machine one doodle at a time. The doodle can be an alphabet, shapes, smiley face, etc. Take one picture of you doodle for each class and hit "train" for the machine to learn. It's that easy! Make sure the doodle is entirely inside the red square. (psst!, a document camera provides best results) Once trained, hit "predict" and show any of your previous three doodle and see if it can recognize it correctly.
